What is Data Science?

Data Science is a multidisciplinary field focused on extracting meaningful insights from data by leveraging statistics, machine learning, and programming. It enables organizations to make informed decisions through robust data analysis, predictive modeling, and advanced visualization techniques. By transforming raw data into actionable intelligence, Data Science drives innovation, efficiency, and strategic growth across industries.
